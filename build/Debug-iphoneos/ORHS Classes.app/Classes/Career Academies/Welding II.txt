Description[[[ Welding II is a course designed to follow Introduction to Welding, in which students will learn more advanced skills and knowledge related to cutting and welding applications. Development of welding and cutting skills will be continued in the context of a series of projects. Combined with the third year course, Advanced Welding applications and Certification, the student should be prepared for Entry Level Welding Certification, as defined by American Welding Society QC10. Students will take certification tests for various welding processes and positions. Students that complete the three course sequence should be prepared for Entry Level Welding Certification, as defined by American Welding Society, QC10.
