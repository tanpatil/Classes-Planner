Description[[[ This course is designed for the student who is interested in art as it relates to photography. The student will study an overview of the history of photography and understand the mechanics of both 35 MM film and digital cameras. Students will study composition and design and apply this knowledge to the taking of photographs. Students learn the basic techniques of developing and printing of black and white film and photos. As students develop basic skills of working a camera and in a darkroom we will begin a creative manipulation of photographs both digitally andmanually.
