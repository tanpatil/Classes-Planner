Description[[[ This course begins a four-year progression of literary analysis in various literary genres for students with aboveaverage reading and writing abilities and/or teacher recommendations. Students taking this course practice rigorous thinking and writing skills necessary for their future success at the Honors/Advanced/AP level and are expected to read and write extensively, often outside of class. Students will also complete an extensive literary research project, plan and write in expository, argumentative, and narrative modes, and review the principles of grammar and language.
