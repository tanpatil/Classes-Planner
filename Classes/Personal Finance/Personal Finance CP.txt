Description[[[ Personal Finance is designed to provide students with a practical study of how individual choices directly influence occupational goals and future earnings potential. Real world topics covered will include income, money management, spending and credit, as well as saving and investing. Students will design personal and household budgets, simulate use of checking and savings accounts, demonstrate knowledge of finance, debt, and credit management, and evaluate and understand insurance and taxes. There will also be an emphasis placed upon current events to ensure that students are familiar with important global and domestic economic issues.
